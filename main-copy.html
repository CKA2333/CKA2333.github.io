About Me

Hi! I am Qifeng Yang, an undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle. Currently, I conduct research at the Wearable Intelligence Lab, where I focus on developing novel technologies at the intersection of electrical & computer engineering and human-computer interaction (HCI).

My research interests include hardware and firmware engineering, human-computer/robot interaction, and wearable devices. I'm passionate about designing reliable, human-centered systems that transform sensor data into actionable feedback.
